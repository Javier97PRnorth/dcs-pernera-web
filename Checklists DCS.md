# Checklists DCS

## TF-51D / P-51D Mustang - Checklist

### Checklist
- Raise the flaps
- Set the carburetor to RAM AIR
- Set RPM to maximum
- Set the parking brake
- Turn on the battery and generator
- Turn on the fuel pump
- Open the fuel flow
- Set magnetos to BOTH
- Prime the engine for 2-5 seconds
- Press the starter and move mixture to RUN when ignition occurs
- Warm up the engine at 1200 RPM until coolant exceeds 60 °C
- Set rudder trim to 5° to the RIGHT
- Set elevator trim to NH 2° when unloaded
- Set elevator trim to NH 4° when loaded
- Close the canopy and unlock the altimeter
- Set the radio to REM and select the radio channel
- Open oxygen
- Turn on the Pitot heater
- Raise flaps when unloaded
- Set flaps to 20° when loaded
- Lock the tailwheel after taxi
- Hold the stick fully back and maintain it until reaching 100 mph

### Engine Management
- Máximo WEP: 3000 RPM | 67 in/Hg | 5 minutos
- Take off: 3000 RPM | 61 in/Hg | 5 minutos
- Combat power: 3000 RPM | 61 in/Hg | 15 minutos
- Máximo continuo: 2700 RPM | 46 in/Hg | Régimen continuo
- Economy cruise: 2400 RPM | 36 in/Hg | Consumo eficiente

## P-47D Thunderbolt - Checklist

### Checklist
- Open the carburetor cold air
- Set the fuel pump rheostat to START/ALTITUDE
- Set the fuel tank selector to MAIN
- Set the parking brake
- Turn on the generator
- Raise the RPM selector switch
- Turn on the battery
- Set the intercooler and oil cooler to NEUTRAL
- Set RPM to maximum and open the throttle 1"
- Prime the engine 2-6 times
- Set magnetos to BOTH
- Set the starter to ENERGIZE for 15 seconds
- Switch the starter to ENGAGE and set mixture to AUTO RICH when ignition occurs
- Warm up the engine at 900 RPM until cylinder head temperature reaches 100 °C, then increase to 1200 RPM
- Open the engine cooling flaps
- Unlock the artificial horizon
- Radio to REM and choose channel
- Pitot heater on
- Set aileron trim to N
- Set rudder trim to T.O
- Set elevator trim to N when unloaded
- Set elevator trim to 0.75" NH when loaded
- Raise flaps when unloaded
- Set flaps to 20° when loaded
- Lock the tailwheel after taxi

### Engine Management
- Máximo WEP: 2800 RPM | 64 in/Hg | 15 minutos con MW
- Take off: 2800 RPM | 52 in/Hg | 5 minutos
- Combat: 2800 RPM | 52 in/Hg | 15 minutos
- Máximo continuo: 2550 RPM | 42 in/Hg | Régimen continuo
- Economy cruise: 2250 RPM | 32 in/Hg | AUTO LEAN

> A partir de 10.000 pies, abrir la palanca del supercargador sin superar 22.000 RPM del supercargador.

## F4U-1D Corsair - Checklist

### Checklist
- Turn on the battery
- Unlock the artificial horizon
- Set the fuel selector to RESERVE
- Set RPM to maximum
- Open the throttle 1"
- Turn on the fuel pump
- Prime the engine for 7-10 seconds
- Set magnetos to BOTH
- Hold the brakes and uncover the starter
- Press until ignition occurs
- Set mixture to AUTO RICH
- Warm up the engine at 900 RPM until oil pressure begins to drop
- Increase to 1200 RPM and close engine cowl and oil cooling flaps
- Wait until oil is 40 °C and head cylinders 120 °C
- Open engine cowl and oil cooling flaps to 1/3
- Turn radio on
- Pitot heater on
- Start lowering the wings and push the wing lock handle
- After wings are down, pull wing lock handle
- Set aileron and rudder trim to 6° right
- Set elevator trim to NH 1°
- Set flaps to 0° when unloaded (from runway)
- Set flaps to 20° when loaded (from runway)
- Set flaps to 50° when launching from carrier
- Lock the tailwheel after taxi
- Set mixture to AUTO LEAN after takeoff

### Engine Management
- Máximo WEP: 2700 RPM | 58 in/Hg | 5 minutos, AUTO LEAN, inyección de agua
- Take off: 2700 RPM | 54 in/Hg | 5 minutos, AUTO RICH
- Combat power: 2700 RPM | 52 in/Hg | 30 minutos, AUTO LEAN
- Máximo continuo: 2550 RPM | 49 in/Hg | AUTO LEAN
- Economy cruise: 2200 RPM | 36 in/Hg | AUTO LEAN

> A partir de 5.000 pies, supercargador a LOW; a partir de 20.000 pies, a HIGH.

## BF-109 K4 Kurfürst - Checklist

### Checklist
- Turn on the battery and generator
- Set the radiators to AUF
- Request wheel chocks
- Activate fuel pumps P1+P2
- Set magnetos to M1+M2
- Turn on ignition
- Open the clutch guard
- Request the starter and prime the engine 2-6 times
- Engage the clutch until the engine starts
- Warm up the engine at 2000 RPM until coolant exceeds 50 °C
- Set propeller RPM to the 12:00 position
- Turn on the Pitot heater
- Remove wheel chocks
- Close the canopy and open oxygen
- Set radio to ON and IFF
- Set trim to +1° unloaded and +2° loaded
- Set radiators to AUTOMATIK
- Lock steering wheel after taxi
- Set the governor to automatic after takeoff

### Engine Management
- Máximo WEP: 2800 RPM | 1.75 ATA | 10 minutos con MW-50
- Take off: 2600 RPM | 1.35 ATA | 5 minutos
- Combat power: 2600 RPM | 1.35 ATA | 30 minutos
- Máximo continuo: 2400 RPM | 1.25 ATA | Régimen continuo
- Economy cruise: 2000 RPM | 1.05 ATA | Consumo eficiente

## FW-190 A8 Anton - Checklist

### Checklist
- Open the button panels and press all switches axcept IFF killer
- Activate navigation lights only if needed
- Request wheel chocks
- Open the fuel shutoff lever
- Turn on E13, E14 and E96 switches
- Turn on E16 if an external tank is installed
- Prime the engine 1-15 times until proper fuel pressure is reached
- Confirm that the fuel indicator shows yellow
- Set magnetos to M1+M2
- Set throttle to START-IDLE and RPM selector to manual at the 12:00 position
- Close the engine cowls
- Uncover the starter and press for 25 seconds
- Pull the starter to engage the clutch until the engine starts
- Warm up the engine at 600 RPM until oil pressure indicator starts incresing
- Increase to 1200 RPM until oil temperature exceeds 25 °C
- Increase to 1500 RPM until oil pressure exceeds 50 °C
- Open engine cowls as necessary
- Set flaps to takeoff position
- Close the canopy and open oxygen
- Uncover landing gear rise cover
- Remove wheel chocks and leave the governor in automatic after takeoff

### Engine Management
- Take off y WEP: 2700 RPM | 1.4 ATA | 3 minutos
- Combat power: 2400 RPM | 1.3 ATA | 30 minutos
- Máximo continuo: 2300 RPM | 1.2 ATA | Régimen continuo
- Economy cruise: 2200 RPM | 1.1 ATA | Consumo eficiente

## Mosquito FB Mk VI - Checklist

### Checklist

- Set the parking brake
- Turn on the battery
- Select the outer fuel tanks
- Open the fuel shutoff valves
- Open the pressure valve and close the fuel transfer valve
- Open the throttles 0.5"
- Set RPM to maximum
- Mixture to RICH
- Open the side window
- Request engine priming
- Turn on magnetos 1 and 2 for both engines
- Uncover and press the starters until the rotors make 3 revolutions
- Activate the booster coils until ignition occurs
- Open radiators flaps and air carburetor air filter
- Warm up both engines at 1200 RPM
- Wait until oil temperature exceeds 20 °C and coolant reaches 40 °C
- Close the window
- Open oxygen
- Set the radio to REM*
- Turn on radio indicating compass (RI compass) and inverter 106A
- Flaps to 15°
- Turn on the Pitot heater
- Turn on R 3078 IFF
- Lights as necessary
- Turn on immersed fuel pump if using long-range fuselage fuel tanks
- Set elevator trim 0.5 divs nose down unloaded and no flaps
- Set elevator trim 2 divs nose down unloaded and with flaps
- Set elevator trim 2.5 divs nose down loaded and with flaps
- Rudder trim 0.5 div right
- Lock the tailwheel after taxi

## I-16 Ishak - Checklist

### Checklist
- Request ground electrical power
- Request wheel chocks
- Close the cockpit doors
- Open the engine cooling flaps
- Set mixture to AUTO RICH
- Set RPM to maximum and open the throttle 1"
- Turn on the battery, generator, and radio
- Open the fuel valve
- Prime the engine 5 times
- Pump fuel to the carburetor for 3 seconds and then to the engine for 3 seconds
- Set magnetos to 1+2
- Pull the inertial starter for 8 seconds, then push to engage the clutch
- Warm up the engine at 1200 RPM until oil reaches 50 °C
- Open cooling flaps if necessary
- Disconnect ground electrical power
- Remove wheel chocks
- Pitot heater
- Check external lights
- Lock the tailwheel after taxi
- Brake the wheels during takeoff before raising the landing gear

### Engine Management
- Máximo WEP: 2300 RPM | 910 mmHg | 5 minutos
- Take off: 2300 RPM | 700 mmHg | 4 minutos
- Máximo continuo: 2200 RPM | 640 mmHg | Régimen continuo
- Economy cruise: 1800 RPM | 500 mmHg | Consumo eficiente

> A partir de 3.000 metros, supercargador a segunda marcha.

## Lavochkin LA-7 - Checklist

### Checklist
- Request connection of ground air supply
- Request wheel chocks and open the fuel shutoff valve
- Close the oil and engine cooling flaps
- Request application of ground air pressure
- Open the aircraft air supply valve
- Prime the engine until reaching 1 kg/cm²
- Open the fuel valve and fill it using the primer handle
- Set RPM to maximum and open the throttle 1 cm
- Turn on the battery and open the air feed to the ignition pump
- Wait for two rotor revolutions
- Turn on the magnetos and press the starter buttons
- Pump fuel using the manual pump
- Maintain fuel pressure until the engine runs steadily
- Warm up the engine at 1000 RPM
- Increase to 1200 RPM until oil exceeds 50 °C and cylinder heads reach 140 °C
- Open oil and engine cooling flaps as needed
- Disconnect ground air supply and remove wheel chocks

### Engine Management
- Combat power: 2300 RPM | 870 mmHg | 1 hora
- Máximo continuo: 2000 RPM | - | Régimen continuo
- Economy cruise: 1600 RPM | - | Consumo eficiente

> A partir de 4.500 metros, supercargador a segunda marcha; no exceder 1000 mmHg en esta configuración.

## F/A-18C Hornet - Checklist Cold Start quick

### Checklist
- Battery switch ON
- Parking brake SET
- APU ON
- Close canopy
- Engine crank RIGHT
- At 21% RPM release right stick
- Repeat for left engine
- Power up MFDs, HUD and HMD if applicable
- Artificial horizon
- Radar OPR
- INS NAV
- HSI select STD HDG (Storage Heading)
- Oxygen Flow ON
- FCS Reset
- Flaps HALF
- Hook Bypass FIELD/CARRIER
- FCS BIT test
- When complete, press T/O Trim
- Configure IFF, TACAN, ILS, Data Link
- Radar altimeter ON
- Arm ejection seat
- FLIR STBY (if applicable)
- When INS indicates OK, switch to NAV or IFA
- RWR, ECM, dispensers ON (in air)
- Taxi light ON
- Parking brake RELEASE

## F-14 Tomcat - Checklist Cold Start quick

### Checklist
- Connect external power and external air
- Turn on oxygen and arm ejection seat
- ICS com check with Jester
- Close canopy
- Engine crank RIGHT, wait 20% RPM, throttle to IDLE
- Engine crank LEFT, repeat
- Air source BOTH ENGINES
- Disconnect external power and external air
- Configure UHF/TACAN radios to T/R
- Turn on AFCS
- Turn on VDI, HUD, HSD; HSD → TID
- Request INS alignment from Jester
- Turn on RWR
- Reset altimeter and radar altimeter
- Uncage standby attitude
- Parking brake RELEASE
- Wing sweep FULL FWD, cover panel
- Master Reset
- Flaps as needed

### Checklist

## MiG-29 Fulcrum - Checklist Cold Start

### Checklist
- Ground electric power ON
- Battery ground supply ON
- Electric power switches (6 switches) ON
- Radio, ACFT, GIRO STBY, NAVIGATION, ACS, WEAPON, RECORD, IFF: ON
- AFCS: OFF
- Wait 40 seconds
- Press Magnetic Heading Slave (key J) and Compass Zero (key 9) for ~10 seconds
- Run Ekran self-test
- Oxygen OPEN
- Close canopy
- Check canopy pin flush (canopy fully locked)
- Navigation switch → OPER
- Arm ejection seat
- Engine start-up switch → BOTH
- Left & right throttles → IDLE
- Press engine start button
- Wait until both engines stabilize at 58-72% idle RPM
- Disconnect ground power
- AFCS ON
- Do not touch the stick while joystick damper performs self-test
- Wait until AFCS green light is steady
- Set joystick to neutral position
- Pitot heat ON
- RWR ON
- Taxi lights ON

## F/A-18C Hornet - Takeoff Procedures

### Shore Takeoff (Essential)
- Anti-Skid ON
- Flaps HALF
- Launch Bar RETRACTED
- Parking Brake RELEASED
- Taxi speed below 20 knots
- NWS LOW gives plus/minus 16 degrees
- NWS HI gives plus/minus 75 degrees (hold Undesignate / Nosewheel Steer Switch)
- Line up on runway
- Hold brakes
- Advance throttles to 80 percent RPM
- Release brakes
- Set throttles to MAX afterburner
- Rotate at approximately 150 knots
- Target pitch is 7 degrees angle of attack
- Positive climb
- Raise landing gear before reaching 240 knots
- Set flaps to AUTO
- Set ALTITUDE switch to BARO at 3000 feet

### Carrier Takeoff (Essential)
- INS alignment in CV mode
- After alignment set INS to IFA if GPS available or NAV if not
- Anti-Skid OFF
- Launch Bar RETRACT
- Hook Bypass CARRIER
- Parking Brake RELEASED
- Taxi with wings folded
- Spread and lock wings before catapult hookup
- Flaps HALF
- Speed Brake IN
- Trim for catapult according to weight
  - 44000 lbs and below: 16 degrees nose up
  - 45000 to 48000 lbs: 17 degrees nose up
  - 49000 lbs and above: 19 degrees nose up
  - If weight 43000 lbs or above and expecting 10 knots or less excess endspeed: add 3 degrees nose up
- Launch Bar EXTEND
- Nosewheel steering disconnects automatically
- If no Supercarrier use U to hook into shuttle
- Run-up
- Throttle MAX afterburner
- If afterburner lockout occurs raise finger lifts (keys 0 and 9)
- Salute with Left Control plus Left Shift plus S
- Catapult shot lasts about two seconds
- FCS sets climb attitude automatically
- Regain control after positive climb
- Raise landing gear before reaching 240 knots
- Set flaps to AUTO
- Perform clearing turn
- Twenty degrees left
- Heading BRC
- Maintain 500 feet and 300 knots
- After seven nautical miles you may climb above 500 feet
- Set ALTITUDE switch to BARO at 3000 feet
